Tirta Susilo | Group Leader
Tirta is senior lecturer in the School of Psychology at Victoria University of Wellington. His research expertise is perception, cognition, and neuroscience, with a focus on face recognition and prosopagnosia. [Homepage] [Email]



Lizzie Collyer | Research Assistant
